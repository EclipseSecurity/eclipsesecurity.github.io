Introducing EclipseSec: Adversary-aware Security

By EclipseSec Team • Jan 28, 2026 • Intro

We design realistic adversary simulations to test detection, response, and resilience — then deliver prioritized, actionable remediation.

Why adversary simulation?

Simulating adversaries helps organizations see how controls perform under realistic conditions. It surfaces gaps that automated scanners and checklists often miss.

Our approach

EclipseSec blends technical penetration testing with threat intelligence and red team tradecraft to craft scenarios tailored to customer risk profiles. We emphasize evidence-driven findings and clear remediation guidance.
